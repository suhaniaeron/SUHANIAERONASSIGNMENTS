IS FAILURE A BLESSING IN DISGUISE????


Failure is simply the opportunity to begin again, this time more intelligently. Quoted by Henry Ford is a quote that shows that failure is a blessing in disguise.
According to the latest forecast of the meteorology department, there will be thundershowers due to my enlightening thoughts. Today, I Suhani Aeron will drench my elite listeners under the shower of my enlightening thoughts on the topic- “Is failure a blessing in disguise?”
According to me, yes! Failure is a blessing in disguise. It is the second opportunity for someone to begin something again but more intelligently. Success and failure are both part and parcel of life. No one can succeed without failing. I have failed and this made me realized that I should pay more attention to overcome my weakness and strengthen my positive qualities.
 Once I made a cake, it became hard and I failed. Next time, I tried again but failed again. But, I didn’t give up and at last, my perfect cake came out from the oven which was tastier than before. If I wouldn’t have failed, then I would not be successful in making the right thing. This shows that failure is a blessing in disguise.
There are many more instances that can be quoted to tell that failure is a blessing in disguise. For example -Walt Disney. Do you know that he was fired from Kansas City Star because his editor felt that he had no good imaginations and thoughts. Also, he was forced to dissolve his company and led on dog food. But now, he’s considered to be the world’s best animators. We can also quote the example of J.K Rowling and many more. This shows that failure is a blessing in disguise.
Last but not the least, our life is full of success and failures. I’m not suggesting that failure is something we should try to do but if you fail, it’s not the end. But if you fail, you should not give up as everything happens for the sake. It depends on us that we take the failure as better or bitter. So, ate the end, is would like to conclude that failure is a blessing in disguise.
